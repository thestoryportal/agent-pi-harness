```

   - Review market conditions
   - Assess competitive landscape
   - Evaluate team performance
   - STOP → Analysis complete

   - Define strategy
   - Allocate resources
   - STOP → Plan developed

3. ALIGN

   - Communicate to team
   - STOP → Alignment complete

   - Monitor progress
   - Adjust as needed
   - STOP → Execution underway

```
TRIGGER: Strategic opportunity identified

   - Assess strategic fit
   - Evaluate deal size
   - Review resource needs
   - STOP → Qualification complete

   - Develop deal strategy
   - Assign resources
   - Define win plan
   - STOP → Strategy set

   - Customer relationships

   - STOP → Engagement active
