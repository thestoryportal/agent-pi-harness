## Problem Statement

We are evaluating a proposed `Operations Research Analyst` role file in our enterprise AI workforce framework, Story Portal. The role must be rated on five quality dimensions, and we need specific, actionable improvements. Two board members have submitted their ratings and findings.

---

## Decision Summary

After reviewing both analyses, I will adopt the direction proposed by **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6**. Their critique is more rigorous, covers all five dimensions in depth, and highlights a critical safety gap—the complete absence of an Anti-Patterns section—while providing highly actionable rewrites for each dimension. This approach minimizes risk, maximizes clarity for AI deployment, and aligns with our bleeding-edge emphasis on precise guardrails for AI-Primary roles.

## Decision Criteria

### Risk
- **Without Anti-Patterns:** High risk of AI-generated correlation-causation errors and scope creep into strategy.  
- **Incorrect Handoffs:** Leads to misaligned downstream workflows and stakeholder frustration.  
- **Insufficient Governance:** Exposes us to operational missteps in mission-critical Story Portal contexts.

### Reward
- **Clarity & Safety:** A robust Anti-Patterns section combined with precise handoff definitions reduces deployment errors.  
- **Efficiency Gain:** Well-defined workflows and STOP checkpoints accelerate safe AI-Primary adoption.  
- **Competitive Edge:** Our roles become exemplars in enterprise AI governance.

### Timeline
- **Week 1:** Draft Anti-Patterns section; refine handoff table.  
- **Week 2:** Integrate improved philosophy principles and AI Deployment checkpoints.  
- **Week 3:** Review & pilot with a live agent in sandbox.  

### Resources
- **HR & Ops Leads:** 2 FTEs for documentation updates.  
- **AI Governance Team:** 1 FTE to validate STOP protocols.  
- **Tech Writer:** 0.5 FTE for formatting and version control.

### Innovation Dimension
As a bleeding-edge CEO, we introduce **“Guardrail Rigor”**:  
- Every AI-Primary role must include a **Failure Mode & Recovery Plan** (explicit STOP points, timeout behaviors, escalations) alongside Anti-Patterns.  
- This raises our standard above generic checklists into actionable, safety-first protocols.

---

## Final Decision

We will follow the comprehensive direction from **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6**, augmented by the practical handoff examples from **openai:o4-mini**. Specifically:
1. **Add a dedicated Anti-Patterns section** with 3–5 role-specific failure modes and mitigation strategies.  
2. **Overhaul the Handoffs table** with precise artifacts, triggers, formats, and version indicators.  
3. **Revise philosophy principles** to embed data-provenance, uncertainty communication, and benchmark validity.  
4. **Embed STOP checkpoints** within workflows and in the Iteration Protocol with explicit human reviewer roles, approvals, and timeout behaviors.  
5. **Enhance Story Portal appendix** with concrete context for festival cycles, system components, and defined metrics thresholds.

This approach minimizes risk, ensures deployable clarity, and up-levels our AI workforce governance.
