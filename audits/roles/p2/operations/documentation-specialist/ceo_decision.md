## Problem Statement  
We tasked our board with evaluating the “Documentation Specialist” role file against a 5-dimension rubric (Philosophy Depth, Handoff Specificity, Anti-Pattern Quality, AI Deployment Clarity, and Story Portal Relevance). Each dimension is scored (1–10) and, for any score below 7, requires a concrete improvement suggestion and example rewrite. The deliverable must be valid JSON per our template.

---

## Executive Summary

This ensures we fully comply with the template standard and mitigate operational risk by strengthening every weak dimension.

## Decision Criteria  

### Risk  
Choosing a partial review (openai:o4-mini) leaves three weak dimensions under-improved, exposing us to inaccurate AI documentation and low Story Portal alignment. Claude-Sonnet’s full-spectrum critique reduces those blind spots.

### Reward  
Implementing Claude-Sonnet’s suggestions will yield a robust role file that:  
- Embeds clear, context-specific philosophy.  
- Anchors handoffs in real artifacts and triggers.  
- Defines anti-patterns to prevent AI-driven mishaps.  
- Surfaces precise AI-agent instructions.  
- Aligns Story Portal deliverables to festival operations.  

### Timeline  
- **Immediate (1–2 weeks)**: Integrate Claude’s example rewrites into the role template.  
- **Next Sprint (2–4 weeks)**: Pilot with an AI agent to validate clarity and handoff flows.  
- **Quarterly**: Audit anti-pattern enforcement and Story Portal usage metrics.  

### Resources  
- **HR & Ops Leadership**: Facilitate rewrite sessions.  
- **AI Engineering**: Update agent configuration.  
- **Festival Program Leads**: Validate Story Portal specificity.  
- **SMEs & Process Managers**: Vet anti-patterns and handoff artifacts.  

### Agent Governance (Novel Dimension)  
As an AI-Primary role, we need explicit guardrails—who signs off on drafts, how unverified gaps are flagged, and metrics for continuous monitoring. Claude-Sonnet’s suggestions lay the groundwork for a governance layer that escalates high-risk content and enforces human checkpoints.

## Final Decision  
Adopt **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6**’s JSON response as the definitive guide. Mandate incorporation of all five dimension-specific improvements and example rewrites into the “Documentation Specialist” role file.
