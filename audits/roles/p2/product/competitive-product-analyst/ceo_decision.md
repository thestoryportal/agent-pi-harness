## Problem Overview

We need to evaluate the “Competitive Product Analyst” role file against our Story Portal enterprise AI workforce framework and rate it on five dimensions. Board members have submitted structured JSON feedback with scores, findings, example rewrites, and top improvement suggestions. Our task is to choose the best direction forward based on their expertise, vote tally, and our CEO-level decision‐making framework.

---

## Executive Summary

After reviewing two valid board responses, I am selecting **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6**’s recommendations as our path forward. Claude’s analysis is more comprehensive—covering handoff specificity, AI deployment clarity, anti-pattern depth, and Story Portal relevance—with actionable rewrites. Their top improvement (handoff specificity) directly addresses the role’s biggest operational gap and delivers immediate, high-impact returns. This choice balances low risk, high reward, rapid timeline, and reasonable resource requirements.

### Reward
- **openai:o4-mini**: Improves conceptual depth, but marginal operational benefit.  
- **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6**: Substantial lift in deployment clarity, team alignment, and immediate execution by AI agents.

### Timeline
- **openai:o4-mini**: Quick win—refine principle table in 1 sprint.  
- **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6**: Medium—rewriting handoff tables, guardrails, Story Portal appendix will span 2–3 sprints but yields end-to-end readiness.

### Resources
- **openai:o4-mini**: Minimal editorial effort by HR/Product leadership.

### Additional Dimensions

1. **Adoption Likelihood**: Claude’s actionable items increase buy-in from implementers—AI developers, analysts, stakeholders.  
2. **AI Deployability**: Clear artifact specs and operational rules directly support agent onboarding.  
3. **Stakeholder Alignment**: Claude’s role-based clarifications ensure each team knows responsibilities, reducing friction.

## Decision

I am endorsing **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6**’s recommendations as the primary blueprint for updating the Competitive Product Analyst role file. We will incorporate their top improvement—**rewriting the Handoffs section**—and address their secondary improvements to philosophy, AI guardrails, anti-pattern specificity, and Story Portal appendix.

---

## Rationale

1. **High Impact**: Redefining handoffs solves the urgent “last-mile” problem—ensuring intelligence reaches the right people in the right format at the right time.  
2. **Improved Deployability**: AI agents and human teams need precise artifact definitions and guardrails; Claude’s approach delivers that.  
3. **Organizational Alignment**: Mapping every deliverable to a chartered role and format minimizes confusion across Product, Marketing, Sales, and CPO.  
4. **Risk Mitigation**: Clear ethical guardrails and fallback protocols reduce legal/operational exposure.  
5. **Strategic Fit**: Aligns the role file sharply with Story Portal’s needs, making competitive monitoring actionable for our festival-specific context.
