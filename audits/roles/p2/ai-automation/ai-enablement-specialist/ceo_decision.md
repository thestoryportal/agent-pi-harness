## Decision Framework

### Risk
- **Without anti-patterns:** High risk of AI agents “hallucinating” workflows, skipping STOP points, or overselling AI capabilities.  
- **Context gaps:** Unspecified context items and blank skills table create a compliance and security risk when agents load the role.

### Reward
- **Claude’s fix:** Instantly elevates role maturity, reduces failure modes, aligns training to enterprise standards, and accelerates autonomous deployment.  
- **Adoption velocity:** With specific handoffs and context, teams hit the ground running.

### Timeline
- **Immediate (1 week):** Draft and embed Anti-Patterns and fill placeholders.  
- **Short (2–3 weeks):** Review and QA with HR, CAO, and Story Portal governance.  
- **Medium (1 quarter):** Monitor adoption metrics and iterate.

### Resources
- **Internal SMEs:** HR for document control, CAO for strategic inputs, AI Ethics Specialist for STOP-point validation.  
- **Tooling:** Notion/Confluence for versioned role files, Slack channels for deliverable triggers.  
- **Budget:** Minimal—content updates and review cycles; no new headcount.

### Innovation Index  *(New Dimension)*
- **Autonomy Readiness:** Measures how AI-first agents can self-execute the role file end-to-end.  
- **Resilience Score:** Rate of role performance recovery after STOP failures.  
- Claude’s plan improves both, moving us from *scripted* to *resilient* and *autonomous* role execution.

---

## CEO Decision

I endorse **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6** as the guiding blueprint. Its holistic approach—detailed anti-patterns, concrete handoff formats, filled context requirements, and Story Portal tie-ins—addresses our highest-risk gaps. We will:

1. **Implement Claude’s Anti-Patterns** verbatim, adapting to our corporate terminology.  
2. **Populate the Context Requirements** table with referenced charter documents.  
3. **Revise Handoffs** to name artifact formats, triggers, and version control.  
4. **Enrich the Story Portal Appendix** to reflect our enterprise framework and link to role-specific workflows.
