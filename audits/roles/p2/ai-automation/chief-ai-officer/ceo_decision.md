## Problem Statement

We tasked the board with reviewing the **Chief AI Officer** role file from our Story Portal enterprise AI workforce framework. The role must be scored across five dimensions and receive concrete, actionable recommendations for improvement. Two valid board responses were submitted:

- **openai:o4-mini**  
- **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6**

Our objective is to select the best guidance, balancing clarity, coverage, and practical next steps.

---

## Quick Summary of Decision

After evaluating both submissions, I select **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6** as the lead recommendation. Its analysis is the most comprehensive—addressing all five dimensions in depth, providing targeted example rewrites, and identifying a clear top-priority gap (the missing anti-patterns section). This level of actionable detail will accelerate our updates and mitigate the greatest risks in the current role file.

## Decision-Making Framework

### Risk
- **Missing Anti-Patterns**: Without role-specific failure modes, incumbents may drift into “ethics theater” or “roadmap optics.”  
- **Hallucinations in Handoffs**: Vague artifacts risk misalignment and wasted effort.  
- **Shallow Story Portal Links**: Lack of concrete deliverables can slow down product teams and introduce compliance gaps.

### Reward
- **Comprehensive Role File**: A best-in-class CAIO role document enhances strategic clarity and accelerates AI adoption.  
- **Guardrails Against Failure**: Anti-patterns and precise handoffs cut down on missteps, improving governance and ROI.  
- **Stronger Alignment**: Tighter Story Portal integration drives immediate business impact on audio processing, accessibility, and safety.

### Timeline
- **Immediate (1–2 weeks)**: Incorporate anti-pattern section, adjust philosophy depth, and refine handoffs per Claude’s examples.  
- **Short-Term (1 month)**: Update AI deployment notes with tooling specs and STOP-point templates.  
- **Medium-Term (2–3 months)**: Roll out updated role file, run pilot training with departments, and measure adoption metrics.

### Resources
- **HR & Executive Leadership**: Review and approve updated templates.  
- **AI Ethics Specialist & Research Lead**: Validate anti-pattern entries and Story Portal specifics.  
- **Engineering & Product Teams**: Align on handoff artifacts and deployment tooling.

- **Training & Rollout**: Clear templates and governance checkpoints expedite adoption and reduce back-and-forth.
