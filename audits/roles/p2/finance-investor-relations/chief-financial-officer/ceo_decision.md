## Decision Criteria

### Risk & Compliance  
- Gap in anti-patterns poses a behavioral risk: lack of guardrails can lead to misprioritization, regulatory missteps, or runway mismanagement.  
- Incomplete handoffs increase cross-team confusion and audit risk.

### Reward & Value  
- A thorough role file with specific anti-patterns, handoffs, and AI triggers accelerates onboarding, reduces oversight burden, and improves financial governance.  
- Detailed Story Portal relevance turns context into executable priorities.

### Timeline & Execution  
- Anthropics’s proposal is more work to implement (five dimensions), but yields a single, cohesive rewrite blueprint.  
- openai’s narrower focus would be faster but leaves other dimensions underdeveloped.

### Resource Allocation  
- Both proposals require HR and Finance SMEs to revise the template.  
- anthropic’s deeper rewrite demands coordination across Finance, Legal, and AI Ops teams to vet examples and triggers.

### AI Actionability (New Dimension)  
- Ensuring an AI agent can “load and go” drastically compresses cycle time.  
- anthropic provides an “AI Agent Entry Point” example—crucial for our hybrid deployment strategy.

## Final Decision & Rationale

I appoint **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6** as the winning proposal. Its comprehensive scope and operational depth outweigh the extra implementation effort. By following its recommendations, we will:

1. Embed **CFO-specific anti-patterns** to safeguard against common failure modes.  
2. Enhance **handoff specificity** with artifact formats, triggers, and cadences.  
3. Deepen **philosophy principles** into actionable trade-offs.  
4. Clarify **AI deployment** with explicit entry points and required contexts.  
5. Make the **Story Portal appendix** prescriptive, tying priorities to measurable goals.

This holistic approach minimizes blind spots, accelerates AI-enabled workflows, and ensures our Finance function becomes a best-in-class pilot for the enterprise AI workforce framework.

Thank you to both board members for their insights. Let’s move forward with anthopic’s roadmap and begin cross-functional workshops to implement these improvements within the next sprint.
