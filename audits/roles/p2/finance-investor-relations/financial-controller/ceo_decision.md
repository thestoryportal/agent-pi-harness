## Problem Overview

We tasked our board with evaluating the “Financial Controller” role file against Story Portal’s Role Template Standard. The deliverable: a JSON rating across five dimensions and targeted improvements for any score below 7.

## Decision-Making Framework

### Risk
- **High** if anti-patterns remain undefined: AI may automate faulty reconciliations or silently adjust entries.  
- **Moderate** if handoffs and Story Portal policies stay generic: teams misinterpret timing, formatting, and thresholds.

### Reward
- **Strong** improvement in operational clarity, audit resilience, and AI-agent autonomy with guardrails.  
- **Long-term** boost in finance team velocity with unambiguous standards.

### Timeline
- **Immediate (1–2 weeks):** Integrate missing Anti-Patterns section and refine philosophy principles.  
- **Short (3–4 weeks):** Fill all context placeholders, finalize handoff tables, and update Story Portal appendix.  
- **Ongoing:** Monitor AI-assisted workflows and iterate per updated Iteration Protocol.

### Resources
- **HR & Finance Leadership (2–3 FTE-days)** to author and approve new content.  
- **AI/Automation Team (1 FTE-week)** to operationalize protocol updates and test AI handoff triggers.

### AI Governance Maturity
- Current maturity is mid-level: Hybrid classification without explicit guardrails invites failure modes.  
- Proposed updates will raise maturity to **Level 4 (Structured Control & Audit)**.

### Implementation Complexity
- **Low to Moderate:** Most fixes are content-authoring tasks; no major system changes required.  
- Integration into our existing role management workflow is straightforward.

## Final Decision

Adopt **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6**’s detailed recommendations as the authoritative direction. Their submission best addresses our compliance risks and AI-agent clarity, and provides prescriptive rewrites that HR and Finance can immediately action.
