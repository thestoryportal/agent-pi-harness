## Problem Statement

We solicited structured feedback on the **UX Designer** role template within our Story Portal AI-workforce framework. The ask was to rate the role on five dimensions and propose concrete improvements for scores below 7. Two board members delivered valid assessments. Our goal now is to select the strongest path forward—balancing risk, reward, timeline, resources, and emerging dimensions of decision excellence.

---

## Executive Summary

Both board members agree the anti-pattern section is overly generic and that deeper, role-specific guidance is needed.  
- **openai:o4-mini** focuses narrowly on anti-patterns and gives a high handoff score but limited additional rewrites.  
- **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6** delivers a holistic overhaul: philosophy, handoff specificity, anti-patterns, AI deployment, and Story Portal relevance—all with detailed rewrites.  

Given our strategic emphasis on precision and context-driven AI integration, **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6**’s recommendations best align with our risk profile, resource capacity, and expected time-to-value.

## Decision-Making Criteria

1. **Risk**  
   • Generic templates → misaligned deliverables, user confusion, legal exposure.  
   • Overly detailed rewrites → scope creep, delayed rollout.

2. **Reward**  
   • Clear, role-specific guardrails → faster onboarding, higher quality, fewer reworks.  
   • Deep contextual alignment → improved user satisfaction, lower support burden.

3. **Timeline**  
   • Quick wins (anti-pattern rewrite): 1 week.  
   • Medium work (philosophy & handoff updates): 2–3 weeks.  
   • Full Story Portal appendix overhaul: 4 weeks.

4. **Resources**  
   • UX Lead (0.2 FTE) to draft/write.  
   • Design Research Lead (0.1 FTE) to provide context docs.  
   • Workshop + review sessions (4×1h).

5. **Innovation Leverage** *(New Dimension)*  
   • Embedding “AI Suggests, Human Decides” accelerates hybrid workflows and sets a precedent for other roles.

6. **Strategic Alignment** *(New Dimension)*  
   • Enhancing specificity in our AI-workforce framework directly supports our EVP of differentiated, context-aware AI products.

---

## CEO Decision

I select **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6**’s recommendations as the primary roadmap. Rationale:

- **Comprehensiveness**: Addresses every dimension under threshold with concrete rewrites.  
- **Contextual Precision**: Ties philosophy, handoffs, and anti-patterns directly to Story Portal’s festival spin-and-record use cases.  
- **Future-Proofing**: Establishes a template for other roles on how to integrate AI/human protocols and product-specific insights.  
- **Balanced Effort**: While more expansive than openai:o4-mini’s narrow focus, the added work strongly mitigates downstream misalignment risk.
