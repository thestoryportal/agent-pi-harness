## Problem Statement  
We need to evaluate two board member proposals for rating and improving the "Head of Support" role file from Story Portal. Each response provides scores on five dimensions and specific improvements. As CEO, I must choose the best proposal, justify why, and align next steps around risk, reward, timeline, and resources.

---

## Quick Summary of Decision  
After tallying and comparing both submissions, I select **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6** as the best direction. Its response delivers deeper contextual relevance, highly specific example rewrites, and covers all template requirements—including the critical missing Anti-Patterns section—with actionable protocols tied directly to Story Portal’s unique support challenges.

**Vote Tally:**  
- openai:o4-mini: 1  
- anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6: 1  

**Tie-Breaker Rationale:**  
Tie resolved in favor of the proposal with the greatest contextual fidelity and readiness for immediate implementation—**anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6**.

## Decision-Making Framework

### Risk  
- **openai:o4-mini:** Lower risk through moderate improvements but may leave core gaps, exposing us to inconsistent support and SLA breaches during events.  
- **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6:** Slightly higher perceived change risk due to comprehensive overhaul but dramatically reduces risk of support failures (festival surge, emotional escalation) by pre-defining protocols.

### Reward  
- **openai:o4-mini:** Improves baseline compliance with the template; limited strategic upside.  
- **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6:** Elevates the role file to best-in-class, aligning support with Story Portal’s mission around emotionally-laden user narratives—high reward in user satisfaction and brand trust.

### Timeline  
- **openai:o4-mini:** Quick patchwork (1–2 weeks) but likely require follow-up cycles as gaps become apparent.  
- **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6:** Initial implementation (2–3 weeks), plus one week of stakeholder review—front-loaded effort but single-pass completion.

### Resources  
- **openai:o4-mini:** Minimal: HR and Support leadership alignment.  
- **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6:** Moderate: content SMEs, engineering on-call to define SLAs, festival operations team to co-author surge protocols.

### Contextual Alignment (New Dimension)  
- **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6** demonstrates superior alignment to Story Portal’s unique features (onsite festivals, emotional content, audio preservation) versus a one-size-fits-all support role. This dimension ensures the role file drives differentiated, mission-aligned support.

## Final Decision  
I authorize the **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6** proposal to guide our next iteration of the Head of Support role file. The detailed, Story Portal–centric improvements will:

- Instantly reduce SLA and quality risks (festival and audio crisis protocols).  
- Deliver strategic reward by reinforcing our brand promise to safeguard users’ memories and stories.  
- Be executed within a focused 3–4 week sprint, leveraging cross-functional SMEs.  
- Strengthen our AI/human hybrid model with clear iteration loops and context loading.

**Next Steps:**  
1. Kick off a 2-week working group with HR, Support, Engineering, and Festival Ops to draft the revised role file sections.  
2. Validate new Anti-Patterns and AI Deployment protocols in a pilot simulation.  
3. Finalize and publish v1.1 of the Head of Support role file with stakeholder sign-off.

By choosing anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6, we ensure our enterprise AI workforce framework is both rigorously templated and deeply tailored to Story Portal’s extraordinary mission.
