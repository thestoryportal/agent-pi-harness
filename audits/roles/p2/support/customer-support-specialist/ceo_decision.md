## Overview  
We asked the board to evaluate the **Customer Support Specialist** role file from our Story Portal AI workforce framework across five dimensions and propose top improvements. Two board members responded with structured feedback and example rewrites. As CEO, I must choose the best direction, balancing risk, reward, timeline, resources, and our new dimension of **Emotional Safety** given the emotionally sensitive nature of our product.

---

## Summary of Decision  
After reviewing both sets of recommendations, I will adopt **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6**’s comprehensive approach. Their feedback is deeper across all five dimensions, provides precise, Story Portal–specific rewrites, and mitigates high-impact risks (especially around emotionally charged user interactions). This path offers the greatest alignment with our objectives and the highest return on investment in safety and quality.

---

## Board Member Recommendations

### openai:o4-mini  
- **Top Improvement**: Add a dedicated Anti-Patterns section with 3–5 role-specific anti-patterns.  
- **Strengths**:  
  - Identifies missing Anti-Patterns.  
  - Provides a concise example for handoff specificity and philosophy.  
- **Limitations**:  
  - Focuses only on one dimension in depth.  
  - Lacks thorough guidance on AI Deployment clarity and Story Portal relevance.  
  - Does not tackle other dimensions where the role file scored below 7.

### anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6  
- **Top Improvement**: Add a dedicated Anti-Patterns section with 4–5 Story Portal–specific behavioral failure modes.  
- **Strengths**:  
  - Deep domain specificity in **Philosophy Depth**, **Handoff Specificity**, **AI Deployment Clarity**, and **Story Portal Relevance**.  
  - Supplies concrete example rewrites for each dimension.  
  - Addresses emotionally sensitive scenarios with precise escalation protocols.  
- **Differentiator**: Proposes complete, end-to-end improvements rather than a single fix, reducing ambiguity at every role phase.

---

## Decision Framework

### Risk  
- **openai:o4-mini** reduces some risk by adding Anti-Patterns but leaves ambiguity elsewhere.  
- **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6** mitigates major operational, compliance, and emotional‐safety risks with detailed guardrails.  

### Reward  
- Mini’s suggestions yield moderate gains.  
- Claude’s holistic approach will significantly improve agent performance, user satisfaction, and reduce incident escalations.

### Timeline  
- Both approaches start with low‐effort additions.  
- Claude’s broader scope requires more initial work but avoids re‐work later by covering all weak spots in one go.

### Resources  
- Implementation of Claude’s recommendations can be staged:  
  1. Add Anti-Patterns (1 week)  
  2. Refine handoff artifacts (2 weeks)  
  3. Update iteration protocol and AI clarity (3 weeks)  
  4. Enrich Story Portal appendix (2 weeks)  
- Total ~8 weeks of cross-functional effort (Support, HR, Engineering).

### Emotional Safety (New Dimension)  
- **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6** explicitly guards against trauma amplification by prescribing empathy-first sequences and no premature closures on loss‐related tickets.  
- **openai:o4-mini** does not address emotional safety beyond generic “Empathy First.”

---

## Final Decision  
I select **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6**’s recommendations as the best course of action. Their feedback provides rigorous, role‐specific, and Story Portal–aligned improvements across all five rated dimensions, plus robust emotional‐safety protocols. We will:

1. **Adopt** their Anti-Patterns section with 4–5 Story Portal–specific failure modes.  
2. **Incorporate** their detailed rewrites for Philosophy, Handoffs, AI Deployment, and Story Portal Relevance.  
3. **Execute** the implementation plan over the next 8 weeks, prioritizing emotional‐safety guardrails.

This balanced, comprehensive approach maximizes quality, minimizes risk, and positions our Customer Support Specialist role for immediate and safe AI deployment in the Story Portal ecosystem.
