## Decision-Making Framework

### 1. Risk
- **Risk of Inaction**: Without explicit anti-patterns and filled context, our CSO template remains operationally blind spots—AI agents won’t know what context to load and humans won’t guard against known failure modes.
- **Risk Appetite**: We must mitigate hidden risks in AI deployment (mis-loaded contexts), so we need the most thorough specification.

### 2. Reward
- **Implementation Fidelity**: Claude-sonnet’s proposal ensures the role file becomes immediately actionable, reducing onboarding friction for AI and humans.
- **Strategic Agility**: Explicit anti-patterns plus context definitions accelerate safe experimentation.

### 3. Timeline
- **Short Term (1–2 weeks)**: Fill in context requirements, anti-patterns, and refine handoffs as Claude-sonnet prescribes.
- **Mid Term (1 quarter)**: Validate the updated template in a live strategic cycle (e.g., Love Burn planning).
- **Long Term**: Institutionalize this enriched template across all AI-Hybrid roles.

### 4. Resources
- **People**: CSO, HR, Research Director, and AI Ops team to author and verify new sections.
- **Tools**: Story Portal document repository, version control (Git), internal review workflow.
- **Budget**: Minimal—mainly staff hours.

### 5. Emergent Dimension: Context Operationalization
Beyond risk/reward/timeline/resources, we must measure how quickly and accurately AI agents can adopt new role files (“AI Onboarding Velocity”). Claude-sonnet’s detail on context artifacts directly improves this metric.

---

## Final Decision & Rationale
I adopt **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6**’s recommendation in full. Its critique is more specific to our festival-market, pre-PMF context, and it addresses two critical gaps:  
1. **Anti-Patterns**: Embedding 3–5 CSO-specific failure modes prevents strategic missteps.  
2. **Context Requirements**: Eliminating placeholders ensures AI agents and humans load exactly the right documents, boosting “AI Onboarding Velocity” and reducing errors.

This choice aligns with our risk tolerance, maximizes implementation fidelity, and delivers the highest strategic value on the fastest timeline with minimal resource impact. We will proceed to update the Chief Strategy Officer role template accordingly.
