## Problem Statement  
We tasked our board with rating the **New Ventures Lead** role file on five dimensions (Philosophy Depth, Handoff Specificity, Anti-Pattern Quality, AI Deployment Clarity, Story Portal Relevance) and recommending improvements. Two detailed board responses arrived with differing scores and improvement plans. I must choose which direction to adopt.

---

## Quick Summary of Decision  
After reviewing both proposals, I will adopt **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6**’s comprehensive recommendations. Both board members agree that the biggest gap is the absence of a dedicated **Anti-Patterns** section, but Claude’s response also delivers highly specific rewrites for handoffs, AI clarity, philosophy, and Story Portal context. This level of actionable detail will accelerate implementation and reduce ambiguity for role authors and AI agents alike.

---

## Board Responses & Commentary

- **Strengths:**

- **Weaknesses:**

Given the depth and actionable clarity, I side with **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6**.

## Decision Framework

### Risk  
- **Without clear anti-patterns**, role adopters will fall into recurring pitfalls (e.g., vanity validation, stealth building).  
- **Overly generic guidance** may lead to inconsistent implementation across ventures, risking brand integrity.

### Reward  
- A **rich, domain-specific role template** will streamline onboarding, reduce missteps, and accelerate time-to-value for new ventures.  
- **AI agents** can autonomously progress tasks with minimal human intervention when given explicit prompts and context.

### Timeline  
- **Week 1:** Integrate Anti-Patterns section and philosophy rewrites.  
- **Week 2:** Update handoff templates and AI context tables.  
- **Week 3:** Align Story Portal appendix with new venture-state matrix; circulate for review.  
- **Week 4:** Publish revised role file (v1.1) and train HR + Strategy teams.

### Resources  
- **HR & Strategy Lead** to own content updates (0.5 FTE for 2 weeks).  
- **Technical Writer** to refine markdown and templates (0.2 FTE for 1 week).  
- **DevOps/AI Team** to deploy updated prompts for AI agent support (0.2 FTE for 1 week).

### Innovation Alignment (New Dimension)  
**“Ritual Integrity Assurance”**  
- Ensures every venture iteration maintains Story Portal’s core storytelling ritual.  
- Provides a quantitative “Ritual Integrity Metric” as a go/no-go gate for new domains.

---

## Final Decision  
I approve the adoption of **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6**’s recommendations. We will:  
1. **Add a dedicated Anti-Patterns section** with 4–5 role-specific pitfalls.  
2. **Revise Philosophy Principles** to tie beliefs to measurable criteria and kill signals.  
3. **Enhance Handoff Specificity** with artifact templates, formats, and trigger conditions.  
4. **Define AI Deployment Clarity** by specifying required context, skills, and startup prompts.  
5. **Enrich Story Portal Relevance** by mapping each venture to stage, hypothesis, next experiment, and ritual integrity gate.

This direction balances risk mitigation, maximizes organizational learning, and fully leverages AI capabilities—driving our New Ventures Lead role to best practice status.
