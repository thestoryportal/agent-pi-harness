## Problem Statement
We tasked our board to evaluate the Head of Client Services role file against the Story Portal Template Standard, rating it across five dimensions and proposing targeted improvements. Two board members (openai:o4-mini and anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6) returned valid JSON critiques.

## Executive Summary
After reviewing both responses, I choose **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6**’s direction because it delivers the most comprehensive, role-specific, and actionable improvements—especially the critical Anti-Patterns section that is entirely missing. This choice mitigates compliance risk, accelerates AI-readiness, and anchors the role firmly in our Story Portal context.

## Decision-Making Framework

### 4.1 Risk
- **Non-compliance** with Template Standard on Anti-Patterns exposes practitioners to unguarded failure modes.  
- **AI adoption stalls** without clear context artifacts and triggers, wasting our automation investments.  

### 4.2 Reward
- **High fidelity** role template driving consistent, high-quality client success outcomes.  
- **Scalable AI-assist** workflows that reduce manual overhead and accelerate insights.  
- **Stronger Story Portal alignment** ensures each client type has tailored success metrics and playbooks.  

### 4.3 Timeline
- **Week 1:** Incorporate Anti-Patterns and contextual playbooks.  
- **Week 2:** Populate Required Context and Skills.  
- **Week 3:** Review and pilot updated role with hiring & training teams.  

### 4.4 Resources
- **HR & Client Services Leadership:** Draft and approve Anti-Patterns.  
- **AI Center of Excellence:** Develop context files and AI task definitions.  
- **Story Portal Owners:** Embed playbooks and workflows in the portal.  

### 4.5 Contextual Anchoring (New Dimension)
Evaluates how deeply the role ties to our unique business model (festivals, sponsors, community).  
- **Score Target:** 9/10  

---

## Final Decision
I select **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6**’s proposal as the north star. Its exhaustive, role-specific improvements unlock compliance, AI-readiness, and Story Portal relevance all at once.
