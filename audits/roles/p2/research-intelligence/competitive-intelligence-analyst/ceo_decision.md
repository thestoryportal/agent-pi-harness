## Problem Statement

We were asked to review a **Competitive Intelligence Analyst** role file and rate it on five dimensions, providing specific findings and improvements for any dimensions scoring below 7. Two board members submitted complete analyses; one submission failed. Our task as CEO is to choose the best direction for improving the role file, balancing risk, reward, timeline, resources, and additional dimensions.

---

## Executive Summary

After reviewing the two detailed board responses, I am selecting **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6**’s recommendations as the primary roadmap. While both members agree that anti-patterns are the highest‐priority gap, Claude’s analysis is deeper across all dimensions, offering concrete rewrites not only for anti-patterns but also for philosophy, handoffs, AI deployment, and Story Portal relevance. This holistic approach mitigates risk more effectively, delivers higher reward in content quality, and can be executed within a moderate timeline and resource allocation.

## Decision Criteria

1. **Risk**  
   - Leaving out anti‐patterns poses a high risk of misanalysis cascading into poor strategic decisions.  
   - Vague handoffs and weak Story Portal context risk misalignment with organizational strategy.

2. **Reward**  
   - Filling anti‐pattern and handoff gaps increases report reliability and stakeholder trust.  
   - Enhanced philosophy and context drive more targeted, actionable intelligence.

3. **Timeline**  
   - Anti‐patterns and handoff updates can be implemented in 2–3 weeks.  
   - Story Portal and AI workflow refinements require 3–4 weeks to validate and test STOP criteria.

4. **Resources**  
   - Internal Research leads and Technical Writers can draft and review improvements.  
   - A small cross‐functional task force (Research, HR, Legal) suffices for approvals.

5. **New Dimension – “Guard Rail Efficacy”**  
   - Measures how well the role file prevents cognitive and operational errors when run in AI‐Primary mode.

---

## Final Decision

I concur with **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6**’s holistic roadmap. Consequently, we will:

1. **Primary Focus: Anti-Patterns**  
   - Create a dedicated “Anti-Patterns” section with 4–5 entries, e.g.:  
     - Last Move Fallacy  
     - Job Posting Literalism  
     - Completeness Over Urgency  
     - Mirror-Imaging Risk  

2. **Secondary Improvements**  
   - **Philosophy Depth**: Replace generic principles with role-specific thinking stances (e.g., “Absence Is Also a Signal,” “Quantitative Signal Filtering”).  
   - **Handoff Specificity**: Define named artifacts, triggers, formats, and frequency (e.g., “Weekly Competitive Pulse Report,” “Competitive Battle Card Update”).  
   - **AI Deployment Clarity**: Embed explicit STOP criteria in workflows and iteration protocols (e.g., Tier-1 threat escalation, source confidence thresholds).  
   - **Story Portal Relevance**: Populate with real competitors, monitoring sources, signal definitions, and escalation thresholds tied to Story Portal’s AI workforce framework.

This comprehensive approach addresses the single highest‐risk gap (lack of anti-patterns) while boosting overall clarity and alignment with our organizational charter.
