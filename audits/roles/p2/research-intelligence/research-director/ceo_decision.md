## Problem Statement  
We are evaluating the **Research Director** role file within our Story Portal enterprise AI workforce framework. The template requires rating across five dimensions and calls out a mandatory **Anti-Patterns** section. Both board members identified missing or weak anti-patterns as the highest-priority gap.

---

## Quick Summary  
All valid board responses unanimously point to the lack of a dedicated, role-specific Anti-Patterns section as the single greatest deficiency. Addressing this will:  
- Provide guardrails against common failure modes  
- Enhance role clarity and reduce missteps  
- Increase confidence for both human and AI actors loading the role file  

---

## Board Votes & Analysis

### openai:o4-mini  
- Scores: Philosophy Depth 8; Handoff Specificity 7; Anti-Pattern Quality 1; AI Clarity 6; Story Portal Relevance 7  
- Top Improvement: **Add a dedicated Anti-Patterns section** with 3–5 research-director-specific examples  

### anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6  
- Scores: Philosophy Depth 3; Handoff Specificity 3; Anti-Pattern Quality 1; AI Clarity 4; Story Portal Relevance 5  
- Top Improvement: **Create a complete Anti-Patterns section** to guard against predictable failures in fast-paced, festival-constrained research  

**Tally:** Both board members (2/2) vote for adding Anti-Patterns as top priority.

---

## Decision Criteria

### 1. Risk  
Without explicit anti-patterns, Research Directors may default to generic boundaries, leading to:  
- Analysis paralysis or over-rigor (delayed decisions)  
- Siloed insights (no cross-functional activation)  
- Misaligned participant recruitment  

### 2. Reward  
A concise, tailored Anti-Patterns section will:  
- Significantly reduce common failure modes  
- Improve cross-functional alignment and speed  
- Boost both human and AI agent onboarding to the role  

### 3. Timeline  
- **Drafting & Review:** 3 business days  
- **Executive Sign-off:** 2 business days  
- **Publication & Communication:** 1 day  
_Total: ~1 week_

### 4. Resources  
- **HR & Research Ops** to co-author the section  
- **Head of Org Intelligence** to validate examples  
- **Design/Doc Team** to update template visuals  

### 5. Governance Agility (Invented Dimension)  
Ensuring minimal yet robust guardrails (“Minimum Viable Governance”) empowers rapid iteration without sacrificing role clarity in our hybrid AI-Human model.

---

## Final Decision  
We will **immediately author and integrate a dedicated Anti-Patterns section** into the Research Director role file. This section will include **5 specific failure modes**—drawn from both board suggestions—and **corrective behaviors**.  

Next steps:  
1. Kick off a 2-hour workshop with HR, Research Ops, and Org Intelligence (Day 1)  
2. Draft Anti-Patterns and circulate for feedback (Days 2–3)  
3. Obtain executive approval (Days 4–5)  
4. Publish updated role file and inform all stakeholders (Day 6)  

By prioritizing this enhancement, we mitigate the greatest risk and align our workforce framework to the high-velocity, festival-focused context of Story Portal.
