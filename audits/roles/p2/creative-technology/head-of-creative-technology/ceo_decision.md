## Problem Overview
We tasked our board with evaluating the **Head of Creative Technology** role file against the Story Portal framework and rating it on five dimensions. Two board members responded with scores, findings, and top improvement recommendations. The core question: **Which improvement should we prioritize to maximize clarity, alignment, and AI readiness?**

---

## Summary of Decision
After weighing both proposals, we will **prioritize adding a Hybrid Iteration Protocol** (anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6’s recommendation) to close a key gap in our AI-human workflow. We will also incorporate **role-specific anti-patterns** (openai:o4-mini’s suggestion) as a secondary enhancement. This combined approach ensures both robust AI guardrails and sharper domain specificity.

---

## Board Member Decisions

### openai:o4-mini
- **Top Improvement**: Make anti-patterns more role-specific (e.g., misuse of shaders, asset pipeline neglect).  
- **Rationale**: Current anti-patterns are generic and don’t address Creative Technology pitfalls.  
- **Strength**: Clear example rewrite for shader misuse.  
- **Limitation**: Focuses narrowly on anti-patterns without addressing the missing Hybrid iteration protocol.

### anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6
- **Top Improvement**: Add a Hybrid Iteration Protocol section.  
- **Rationale**: The role is classified Hybrid, but lacks documented AI-human revision loops. Without it, an AI agent has no guardrails on autonomous iteration depth or STOP points.  
- **Strength**: Directly addresses a mandatory template standard (iteration protocol for Hybrid roles) and bolsters AI safety.  
- **Limitation**: Does not explicitly call out anti-pattern specificity, which is also important.

---

## Decision Criteria

### Risk Management
- **Iteration Protocol Gap**: Without clear AI-human breakpoints, we risk uncontrolled AI revisions, quality drift, or misalignment with human judgment.  
- **Anti-Pattern Vagueness**: Generic anti-patterns may lead to overlooked domain-specific failures, but these pose a lower operational risk than missing AI guardrails.

### Reward Opportunity
- **Hybrid Iteration Protocol**: Elevated AI collaboration confidence, faster onboarding of AI agents, and compliance with our own template standards.  
- **Domain-Specific Anti-Patterns**: Sharper team awareness of creative-tech pitfalls, improved quality consistency.

### Timeline & Implementation Plan
- **Iteration Protocol**: Drafting and review within 1–2 sprints (2–4 weeks); minor update to role template and Story Portal documentation.  
- **Anti-Pattern Refinement**: Concurrent 1-week task for Creative Technology leads and HR to craft 3–5 new entries with concrete examples.

### Resource Allocation
- **Core Team**: Head of Creative Technology + AI Governance Specialist to define protocol.  
- **Support**: HR Partner for template edits; Creative Tech SMEs for anti-pattern input.  
- **Estimated Effort**: ~40 hours total across teams — well within quarterly planning.

### AI Safety & Governance (New Dimension)
- **Checkpoint Definition**: Number of AI drafts allowed, required human sign-off per artifact type.  
- **Documentation**: Central “Iteration Protocol” playbook entry, cross-referenced in the AI Assistance Model section.  
- **Ownership**: AI Governance + Head of Creative Technology.

---

## Final Decision & Next Steps

1. **Approve** development of a **Hybrid Iteration Protocol** for the Head of Creative Technology role:
   - Define STOP points, artifact formats, review cycles, and final sign-off authority.
   - Publish in the Role Template under a new “Hybrid Iteration Protocol” section.
2. **In Parallel**, refine the **Anti-Patterns** list to include 3–5 creative-tech-specific pitfalls:
   - Example: “Blindly apply default shader packs — Instead: craft custom shader graphs that balance brand narrative with mobile performance budgets.”
3. **Assign Owners**:
   - Hybrid Protocol: AI Governance Specialist + Head of Creative Technology (2-week sprint).
   - Anti-Patterns: Creative Technology SMEs + HR Partner (1-week sprint).
4. **Verify** updates against the TEMPLATE STANDARD to ensure all 11 sections, iteration protocol, and role-specific content are present.

By prioritizing AI-human workflow clarity and domain specificity, we strengthen both our Story Portal compliance and the operational excellence of the Creative Technology function.
