## Problem Statement

We are assessing the quality of the “WebGL Engineer” role file within our Story Portal enterprise AI workforce framework. The task is to rate the role across five dimensions and identify improvements. Two board members have provided their analyses and top recommendations:
- openai:o4-mini: Enhance the Philosophy section with concrete mini-case examples.
- anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6: Fix handoff specificity by replacing vague artifact names with exact file paths and validating collaborator roles.

---

## Executive Summary

After reviewing both recommendations under our key criteria—risk, reward, timeline, resources, and new dimensions such as Implementation Agility and Human-AI Alignment—I will prioritize **improving handoff specificity**. This change addresses a lower-rated dimension (handoff specificity scored 6 by Claude-Sonnet) and reduces the risk of AI agents failing to locate or deliver artifacts correctly. Once handoffs are rock-solid, we can then iterate on enriching the Philosophy section.

---

## Board Member Recommendations

### openai:o4-mini
- **Scores**: All dimensions 8–9 (excellent).
- **Top Improvement**: Enhance the Philosophy section with real mini-case examples to deepen practical guidance.

### anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6
- **Scores**: One dimension below 7—Handoff Specificity scored 6.
- **Top Improvement**: Replace vague artifact names with exact file paths, formats, and interface contracts; confirm or remove hallucinated “Performance Engineer” and “QA” roles.

---

## Decision Criteria

### Risk
- **Enhancing Philosophy**: Low risk to system operation; mainly documentation updates.
- **Fixing Handoffs**: Medium risk if not done—AI agents or team members could stall or miscommunicate without precise handoff details.

### Reward
- **Enhancing Philosophy**: Increases role depth and mentorship quality.
- **Fixing Handoffs**: Directly improves workflow reliability; highest return on preventing misfires in AI-based executions.

### Timeline
- **Enhancing Philosophy**: 1–2 days of writing and review.
- **Fixing Handoffs**: 1–3 days to audit and update handoffs; parallel verification with charter.

### Resources
- **Enhancing Philosophy**: Involves SMEs for mini-case examples.
- **Fixing Handoffs**: Involves HR, Creative Tech leads, and Technical Writers reviewing artifacts and role lists.

### Additional Dimensions

1. **Implementation Agility**  
   - How quickly can AI and humans begin using the updated role?  
   - Precise handoffs boost immediate usability.

2. **Human-AI Alignment**  
   - Reduces misinterpretation between AI agents and human stakeholders.

---

## Chosen Direction

**Primary Action**: Fix handoff specificity in the WebGL Engineer role file.

**Secondary Action** (Phase 2): Enrich the Philosophy section with concrete mini-case examples.

---

## Rationale

1. **Addressing the Lowest Score First**  
   - Claude-Sonnet identified handoff specificity at 6/10—below our 7-point threshold. This gap poses a tangible risk: AI agents may attempt handoffs to non-existent roles or use wrong artifact formats, causing workflow breakdowns.

2. **High Impact, Reasonable Effort**  
   - Updating handoffs with file paths, component interfaces, and validated collaborator roles directly improves operational clarity. The effort (1–3 days) is modest compared to the benefit.

3. **Enabling Philosophy Enhancements**  
   - Once handoffs are rock-solid, we can confidently layer on richer Philosophy examples without undermining core execution.

4. **Aligning with Story Portal Objectives**  
   - Precise artifact definitions and role mappings ensure our AI workforce functions seamlessly within Story Portal’s hybrid model.

---

I direct HR and the Creative Technology leadership team to draft the updated handoff tables and perform a quick audit against the Organizational Charter. Upon completion and sign-off, we will schedule the Philosophy section enhancements. This two-phase approach balances immediate operational stability with long-term role depth.
