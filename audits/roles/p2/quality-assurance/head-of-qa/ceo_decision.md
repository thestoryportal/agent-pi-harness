## Decision-Making Framework

### Risk Assessment  
- **Without Iteration Protocol**: We violate the template’s “Iteration protocol included for Hybrid/AI-Primary” requirement → potential misalignment, lack of clarity for AI agents, unstructured review loops.  
- **With Iteration Protocol**: Clear boundaries, governance for AI drafting, escalation triggers → mitigated quality-gate failures and bottlenecks.

### Reward Potential  
- **High Compliance**: Fulfilling all checklist items boosts trust in our frameworks.  
- **Operational Efficiency**: Teams and AI agents know exactly when and how to loop in humans, reducing rework and confusion.

### Timeline Impact  
- **Low Effort**: Crafting and inserting an iteration protocol is a focused update (1–2 days for drafting, review, and integration).  
- **Immediate Benefit**: Can be rolled into the next role file iteration without delaying broader QA framework releases.

### Resources Required  
- **Minimal**: A small working group comprising Head of QA, QA Operations Manager, and an AI-Governance SME to draft the protocol.  
- **No Additional Tooling**: Leverages existing documentation and governance processes.

### Compliance Urgency (New Dimension)  
- **Mandatory Checklist Item**: This gap is a “hard stop” in the quality checklist—other suggestions, while valuable, are optional enhancements.  
- **Regulatory/Stakeholder Trust**: Ensuring full adherence to Story Portal standards maintains stakeholder confidence in our governance rigor.

---

## Final Decision  
Implement the **Hybrid Iteration Protocol** as the top‐priority improvement. By doing so, we close a structural compliance gap, provide clear AI-human collaboration rules, and elevate the Head of QA role file to full template conformance. This action delivers immediate risk mitigation, high reward in governance clarity, and requires minimal time and resources. 

We will schedule a 2-hour workshop this week to draft the protocol, finalize it by next Monday, and publish an updated role file by end of sprint.
