## Decision Criteria

As CEO, I evaluate along these axes:

1. **Risk**  
   - Generic or incomplete specs increase misalignment, AI errors, and manual rework.  
   - Missing anti-patterns is the highest risk vector—without guardrails, both humans and AI will default to bad sales behaviors.

2. **Reward**  
   - A role file with specific, mission-driven content accelerates onboarding, strengthens Story Portal positioning, and fuels quota attainment.  
   - Clear AI protocols boost automation ROI.

3. **Timeline**  
   - Adding Anti-Patterns and filling placeholders: low-effort, <1 week.  
   - Full Philosophy and Appendix overhaul: 2–3 weeks, but can be phased.

4. **Resources**  
   - HR & Sales leadership to workshop Anti-Patterns.  
   - Product & Marketing to craft Story Portal messaging matrices.  
   - AI/Engineering to finalize protocol specs.

5. **Adoption Velocity (Invented Dimension)**  
   - Rapid wins (Anti-Patterns) will build momentum for deeper enhancements.

6. **Alignment Synergy (Invented Dimension)**  
   - Claude’s critique is tightly aligned with our community-impact mission and festival partnership model.
