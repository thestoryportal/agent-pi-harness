## Problem Overview  
We tasked our board to evaluate the **Deal Desk Analyst** role template across five dimensions—Philosophy Depth, Handoff Specificity, Anti-Pattern Quality, AI Deployment Clarity, and Story Portal Relevance—and recommend improvements. Two of four board members provided valid analyses. Both converged on a critical omission: a dedicated Anti-Patterns section. Our goal as CEO is to select the strongest board input, synthesize insights, and set the direction for optimizing this AI-Primary role template.

---

## Decision Summary  
After reviewing the votes and depth of analysis:
- **Winner**: anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6  
- **Unanimous Top Improvement**: Add a dedicated, role-specific Anti-Patterns section with clear failure modes and corrective behaviors.  
- **Secondary Improvements**:  
  - Enhance handoff artifact specificity (formats, fields, signals).  
  - Flesh out the Iteration Protocol with explicit thresholds and stop/gate logic.  
  - Solidify Story Portal appendix with actionable, deal-type–specific rules.

## Decision-Making Criteria

### Risk  
- **Low**: Updating a role file in our AI workforce framework carries minimal regulatory or financial risk.  
- **Mitigation**: Peer-review changes to ensure consistency with Organizational Charter.

### Reward  
- **High**:  
  - Prevents unintentional AI behavioral failures.  
  - Strengthens compliance and margin protection.  
  - Reduces escalations and turnaround time errors.

### Timeline  
- **Draft Updates**: 1 week (Role SME + AI Architect)  
- **Review & Approval**: 1 week (Sales Leadership + Finance + Legal)  
- **Deployment**: Immediately upon approval into Story Portal.

### Resources  
- **SME Hours**: ~20 hours total  
- **Engineering**: None (document update only)  
- **Governance**: Review by HR + Sales ops

### New Dimension: Feasibility & Scalability  
- **Feasibility**: High – examples provided can be copy/pasted into the template.  
- **Scalability**: Anti-Patterns and iteration protocol improvements scale to other AI-Primary roles.

## Final Decision & Next Steps  
1. **Adopt anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6’s recommendations** as the baseline—due to their comprehensiveness and actionable detail.  
2. **Implement a dedicated Anti-Patterns section** with at least four role-specific failure modes and corrective behaviors.  
3. **Refine handoff tables** to include artifact formats, required fields, and completion signals.  
4. **Enhance the Iteration Protocol** with explicit thresholds, STOP criteria, and routing logic.  
5. **Update the Story Portal Appendix** into a deal-type matrix with standard terms, discount authorities, and compliance flags.  

**Action Plan:**  
- Assign ownership to the Sales Operations SME and AI Framework Architect.  
- Kickoff workshop this Friday; draft due next Wednesday.  
- Signoff by Sales VP and Finance Director by the end of the following week.  

Let’s proceed with these targeted updates to elevate our Deal Desk Analyst role to best-in-class standards.
