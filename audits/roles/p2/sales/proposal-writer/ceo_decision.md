## Overview

We tasked our board with evaluating the “Proposal Writer” role file against our Story Portal enterprise AI workforce framework. The board provided two complete responses (openai:o4-mini and anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6) and one failed attempt. Our goal as CEO is to choose the recommendation that best balances risk, reward, speed, resource efficiency, and innovative depth.

---

## Executive Summary

After tallying the two valid board responses:

- Both models identified the lack of **role-specific anti-patterns** as the top issue.
- openai:o4-mini gave concise feedback with high clarity on anti-patterns, philosophy depth, and Story Portal relevance.
- anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6 delivered a more exhaustive critique across all dimensions, with concrete rewrites and a robust anti-patterns section proposal.
- **Vote count:** Each model cast one “vote” by their top-improvement recommendation. However, quality of analysis favors anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6.

**Chosen direction:** Adopt the comprehensive anti-patterns framework and improvements outlined by anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6.

## Decision-Making Framework

### Risk
- **Low risk** in adding an Anti-Patterns section.  
- **Moderate risk** if we over-engineer new workflows, delaying rollout.  

### Reward
- **High reward**: Anti-patterns guardrails prevent costly proposal failures.  
- Enriched philosophy and handoff specificity boost proposal quality and agent reliability.

### Timeline
- **Anti-patterns**: draft within 1 week.  
- **Philosophy & Handoffs Enhancements**: incremental updates over 2–3 weeks.  
- **Full Story Portal operationalization**: 4–6 weeks to test and refine.

### Resources
- HR & Sales Leadership: 1–2 days to co-author anti-patterns.  
- AI Engineering: 1 sprint to encode new iteration logic and artifact schemas.  
- Story Portal PM: 1 week to codify appendix guidelines.

### Innovative Depth
- New dimension: **Innovative Depth** measures how a recommendation pushes the envelope beyond mere compliance.  
- anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6 exhibits high innovative depth by adding AI decision trees and operational Story Portal hooks.

---

## Final Decision

I select the **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6** recommendation. Its thoroughness and innovative approach position us not only to fix the missing anti-patterns but to elevate the entire “Proposal Writer” role to a robust, AI-ready, Story Portal-aligned asset.

**Next Steps:**
1. Convene HR + Sales leadership to draft and agree on 4–5 role-specific anti-patterns by end of week.  
2. Align AI Engineering team to update the iteration protocol with decision logic and tone rules next sprint.  
3. Work with the Story Portal team to operationalize the appendix into actionable templates and metrics.  

This path maximizes quality, minimizes risk, and accelerates our time to deploy a best-in-class AI Writing role for Story Portal.
