## Decision Criteria

As CEO, I evaluate strategic recommendations through these lenses:

1. **Risk**  
   - What happens if we underdeliver or misimplement?  
2. **Reward**  
   - What gains in clarity, adoption, and AI reliability can we realize?  
3. **Timeline**  
   - How quickly can we implement key improvements?  
4. **Resources**  
   - Who, what, and how much effort is required?  
5. **Mission Alignment Index** (New Dimension)  
   - How closely do the improvements tie back to Story Portal’s empathy-driven, indirect venue model?  

---

### Risk

- **Minimal** if we follow Claude’s precise examples—avoids AI drift and human misunderstanding.  
- **High** if we only add anti-patterns but leave philosophy and context gaps.

### Reward

- **Substantial** clarity for humans and AI, improved forecasting accuracy, stronger partner relationships, and consistent mission messaging.

### Timeline

- **Immediate (1–2 weeks)** for philosophy and anti-patterns rewrites.  
- **Short term (2–4 weeks)** to finalize handoff templates, AI protocols, and Story Portal appendix.  

### Resources

- **Content Team & Sales Leadership**: 2–3 days to review and approve new philosophy and anti-patterns.  
- **RevOps/RevTech**: 1 week to code AI queries and STOP-point automations.  
- **Marketing & Partnerships**: 1–2 weeks to flesh out partner criteria and deal templates.

### Mission Alignment Index

- Claude’s recommendations map directly to our festival, arts, community, and tech partner personas.  
- Ensures every principle and workflow honor our empathy-driven storytelling ethos.
