## Decision Criteria

### Risk
- **Low anti-pattern guidance** → High chance of missteps (e.g., closing misaligned deals, over-trusting AI forecasts).  
- **Vague handoffs** → Operational friction, handoff errors, delayed deliveries.  
- **Weak AI clarity** → Underutilized or misused AI agents, lost efficiency gains.

### Reward
- **Anti-Pattern Framework** → Reduces failure modes, improves governance, accelerates ramp.  
- **Precise Handoffs** → Smoother cross-functional ops, faster time-to-close.  
- **Deep Philosophy & Story Context** → Stronger alignment with mission, spot-on GTM tactics.

### Timeline
- **Anti-Patterns Section**: 1–2 weeks to draft, review, and finalize.  
- **Handoff & AI Clarifications**: 2–3 weeks, parallel sprints with Marketing, Finance, Engineering.  
- **Philosophy & Story Portal Updates**: 3–4 weeks, iterative refinement with stakeholders.

### Resources
- **Internal**: HR (1 FTE part-time), Sales ops (1 FTE), AI engineer (0.5 FTE).  
- **External**: Consulting support not required; leverages in-house SMEs.  
- **Budget**: Minimal incremental cost—document updates and stakeholder workshops.

### AI/Hybrid Integration
- We need **explicit AI task definitions**, clear **stop points**, and **context-file inventories** so our AI agents can autonomously generate reports, flag risks, and prompt human interventions.

## Final Decision
I endorse **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6** as the lead recommendation. We will implement its holistic improvements, starting with the **Anti-Patterns section**, and cascade the specificity into all other areas of the CRO role file.

## Rationale
1. **Holistic Coverage**: Claude’s response tackles every dimension in depth, not just the top-of-mind issues.  
2. **Mission Alignment**: Example rewrites embed Story Portal’s unique context (festival partnerships, community sponsors, grant funding).  
3. **AI-First Clarity**: Provides the “AI MAY / AI MAY NOT” guardrails and specific context items, ensuring seamless hybrid operations.  
4. **Operational Precision**: Detailed handoff templates and artifact definitions minimize execution risk and speed up cross-team collaboration.  
5. **Cascading Impact**: By adding anti-patterns, we simultaneously sharpen philosophy, workflow stop points, and Story Portal relevance—driving high ROI on a small investment of time and resources.

Implementing Claude’s recommended changes will significantly elevate the CRO role file’s quality, reduce our operational risk, and accelerate our revenue engine in line with Story Portal’s mission-driven, hybrid AI strategy.
