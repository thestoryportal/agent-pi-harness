## Problem Statement  
We reviewed the Sales Director role template against the enterprise Story Portal framework. The task: rate the role on five dimensions and propose targeted improvements. Two board members submitted analyses; one model failed to respond.

---

## Executive Summary  
After evaluating both sets of feedback, **I select the recommendation from anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6**. Claude’s analysis is more granular across dimensions, provides richer, role-specific examples (particularly for handoffs, anti-patterns, and Story Portal relevance), and highlights critical gaps with strong actionable rewrites. The top priority shared by both was to **add a dedicated, role-specific Anti-Patterns section**, but Claude’s broader coverage makes his overall direction superior.

## Decision Framework

### Risk  
- **Low Adoption Risk** if we deliver clear artifacts and anti-patterns.  
- **Medium Risk** that without strong Story Portal context, new Sales Directors misalign with festival-drive motions.  
- **Mitigation:** Leverage Claude’s detailed rewrites to anchor content in real use cases.

### Reward  
- **High Reward** through a bullet-proof role template that drives consistent execution, prevents common failures, and accelerates onboarding of both human and AI agents.  
- **Secondary Reward**: stronger cross-functional alignment (Sales, Marketing, Ops) via precise handoff definitions.

### Timeline  
- **Week 1:** Draft Anti-Patterns, enrich Philosophy, and fill Context Requirements/AI Deployment per Claude’s examples.  
- **Week 2:** Circulate updated draft to HR and Sales Ops for feedback.  
- **Week 3:** Finalize version 1.1 of Sales Director template; deploy in Story Portal.

### Resources  
- **HR Team (1 FTE):** Integrate new sections, update metadata.  
- **Sales Ops (0.5 FTE):** Validate artifact definitions, build reports/templates.  
- **AI Center of Excellence (0.5 FTE):** Configure AI responsibilities and context file load logic.  

### Adoption Velocity  
(Our novel dimension measuring how quickly new hires and AI agents can use this role without friction.)  
- **Current Velocity:** Low – placeholders block AI, anti-patterns missing.  
- **Target Velocity:** High – clear templates, examples, and guardrails enable immediate use.

---

## Final Decision  
Adopt **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6**’s approach as our blueprint. We will:

1. **Insert a dedicated Anti-Patterns section** with at least five Sales-Director–specific failure modes (e.g., hero-Closing, forecast Optimism, Q4 pipeline neglect).  
2. **Revise the Philosophy** with context-rich principles tied to festival partnerships and sponsorship sales.  
3. **Specify handoff artifacts** at the document/field level (e.g., SFDC opportunity record schema, closed-deal handoff package contents).  
4. **Populate AI Deployment and Context Requirements** with real file names, data sources, and triggered AI actions.  
5. **Transform the Story Portal appendix** into an actionable operating guide for each motion: buyer personas, deal sizes, cycles, and current pipeline status.  

By following Claude’s detailed framework and examples, we mitigate risk, maximize reward, and accelerate adoption of the role template across humans and AI. This will be our **Sales Director Role Template v1.1** roll-out plan.
