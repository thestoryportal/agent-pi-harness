## Problem Statement

We are reviewing a Performance Marketing Manager role template from Story Portal’s enterprise AI workforce framework. The task: rate the role on five dimensions, identify gaps, and propose concrete improvements. Two board members (openai:o4-mini and anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6) submitted JSON-based analyses. We must select the best direction.

---

## Quick Summary of Decision

After evaluating both analyses, we adopt **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6**’s recommendations. Claude’s response is the most thorough, prescriptive, and actionable, with detailed example rewrites across all five dimensions and an emphasis on missing anti-patterns—our highest priority gap.

## Decision Criteria

### Risk
Without a clear **Anti-Patterns** section, our Performance Marketing Manager may inadvertently burn budget on vanity metrics, misreport blended CAC, or draw premature conclusions—jeopardizing both spend efficiency and community trust.

### Reward
Implementing Claude’s detailed framework will yield:
- Robust guardrails against common pitfalls  
- Precise handoff artifacts and formats  
- An AI-deployment blueprint that accelerates onboarding of autonomous agents  
- Story Portal–specific context that drives more relevant campaigns

### Timeline
1–2 weeks to update the role template, including:
- Anti-patterns section  
- Revised handoffs with schema and cadence  
- Expanded AI iteration protocol  
- Story Portal context table  

### Resources
- 1 cross-functional workshop (Marketing, Analytics, HR)  
- 1 technical writer for template updates  
- 1 week of stakeholder review  

### Narrative Clarity (New Dimension)
We must ensure the role narrative ties directly to Story Portal’s mission of community-driven storytelling. Claude’s contextual table provides the richness needed for both human and AI stakeholders to align messaging with platform values.

---

## Final Choice

We select **anthropic:claude-sonnet-4-6**’s proposal as our blueprint. Its comprehensive, role-specific anti-patterns, concrete handoff definitions, and deep operational detail best position Story Portal to deploy a hybrid AI-human Performance Marketing Manager that maximizes ROI while safeguarding community health.
